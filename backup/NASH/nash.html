Steve Nash

Canada's greatest assist to the NBA

Steve Nash is the only Canadian NBA player to win not one but two regular season MVPs.

Here are all of Steve Nash's accomplishments in the NBA:

  • All-Star
    • - 8 times (2002~2003,2005~2008,2010,2012)
